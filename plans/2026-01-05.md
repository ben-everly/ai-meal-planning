## Weekly Highlights

**Snack 2:** Sliced Pears with Almond Butter

**Dessert:** Baked Cinnamon Apples

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 4-5 Apples, cored and sliced
    - [ ] 1 tsp Cinnamon
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Coconut oil
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Maple syrup (for adults only)
- **Preparation:**
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
    2. Toss apples with cinnamon and coconut oil.
    3. Bake for 20-25 minutes until soft.
    4. Serve plain to baby; drizzle with maple syrup for adults.

## Daily Meals

## Monday

**Breakfast:** Oatmeal with Almond Butter

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Rolled oats
    - [ ] 1 cup Water or Cashew milk
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Almond butter
- **Preparation:**
    1. Cook oats in liquid for 5-7 minutes.
    2. Stir in almond butter.

**Lunch:** Tuna Salad with Whole Wheat Crackers

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 can Low-sodium tuna
    - [ ] 1/2 Avocado (mashed)
    - [ ] 1 tsp Lemon juice
    - [ ] Whole wheat crackers (low sodium)
- **Preparation:**
    1. Mix tuna, avocado, and lemon.
    2. Serve with crackers.

**Dinner:** Shrimp Scampi with Zucchini Noodles

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1/2 lb Shrimp, peeled and deveined
    - [ ] 2 Zucchinis, spiralized
    - [ ] 2 cloves Garlic, minced
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Olive oil
    - [ ] Lemon juice
- **Preparation:**
    1. Sauté garlic in oil for 1 min.
    2. Add shrimp and cook for 3-4 mins.
    3. Add zucchini noodles for the last 2 mins. Finish with lemon.
- _Note:_ Prep for smoking pork shoulder on Tuesday.

## Tuesday

**Breakfast:** Greek Yogurt with Fresh Berries

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 cup Plain Greek yogurt
    - [ ] 1/4 cup Mixed berries
- **Preparation:**
    1. Mash berries and stir into yogurt.

**Lunch:** (Leftovers from last week)

**Dinner:** Smoked Pork Shoulder

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 4-5 lb Pork shoulder
    - [ ] Salt-free dry rub (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, pepper)
- **Side**: Mashed Sweet Potatoes and Steamed Broccoli
- **Preparation:**
    1. Smoke pork shoulder throughout the day until internal temp reaches 195-205°F.
    2. Shred pork.
    3. Reheat prepped sweet potatoes and steam broccoli (5 mins).

## Wednesday

**Breakfast:** Scrambled Eggs

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 2 Eggs
    - [ ] 1 tsp Olive oil
- **Preparation:**
    1. Whisk and scramble eggs in oil (3-5 mins).

**Lunch:** Pork Tacos

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] Leftover shredded pork
    - [ ] Whole wheat tortillas
    - [ ] Sliced avocado
- **Preparation:**
    1. Warm pork and tortillas.
    2. Assemble with avocado.

**Dinner:** Pork and Cheese Quesadillas

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] Leftover shredded pork
    - [ ] Whole wheat tortillas
    - [ ] 1/4 cup Shredded cheese (low sodium)
- **Preparation:**
    1. Place pork and cheese on tortilla.
    2. Fold and toast in a pan for 2 mins per side until cheese melts.

## Thursday

**Breakfast:** (Eat Out)

**Lunch:** Pork and Quinoa Bowl

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] Leftover shredded pork
    - [ ] 1 cup Prepped quinoa
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Natural peanut butter
    - [ ] 1 tsp Coconut aminos (or soy sauce)
- **Preparation:**
    1. Mix peanut butter and aminos with a splash of water to make sauce.
    2. Toss with pork and quinoa.

**Dinner:** Baked Cod with Roasted Carrots

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 2 Cod fillets
    - [ ] 1 bunch Carrots, peeled and sliced
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Olive oil
    - [ ] 1 tsp Dried parsley
- **Preparation:**
    1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
    2. Toss carrots with oil and roast for 15 mins.
    3. Add cod to the tray, season with parsley, and bake for another 10-12 mins.

## Friday

**Breakfast:** Peanut Butter Toast

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 slice Whole wheat bread
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Natural peanut butter
- **Preparation:**
    1. Toast bread and spread peanut butter.

**Lunch:** (Leftovers from Thursday Dinner)

**Dinner:** (Eat Out)

## Saturday

**Breakfast:** Banana Pancakes

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 Ripe banana
    - [ ] 1 Egg
    - [ ] 2 tbsp Whole wheat flour
- **Preparation:**
    1. Mash banana, whisk in egg and flour.
    2. Cook small pancakes on a greased pan (2 mins per side).

**Lunch:** (Eat Out)

**Dinner:** Tofu Stir-fry #dinner

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 block Firm tofu, cubed
    - [ ] Mixed veggies (Bok choy, carrots, bell peppers)
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Sesame oil
    - [ ] 1 tsp Sesame seeds
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Soy sauce (Coconut aminos for baby)
- **Preparation:**
    1. Sear tofu in sesame oil until golden.
    2. Add veggies and stir-fry for 5-7 mins.
    3. Toss with soy sauce and sesame seeds.

## Sunday

**Breakfast:** Chia Seed Pudding

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 2 tbsp Chia seeds
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Cashew milk (prepped Sunday)
- **Preparation:**
    1. Stir prepped pudding and serve with fruit.

**Lunch:** (Leftovers from Saturday Dinner)

**Dinner:** Lemon Herb Chicken with Roasted Potatoes

- **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 lb Chicken thighs
    - [ ] 3-4 Potatoes, chopped
    - [ ] 1 tsp Dried rosemary
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Olive oil
- **Preparation:**
    1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
    2. Toss chicken and potatoes with oil and rosemary.
    3. Roast for 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
