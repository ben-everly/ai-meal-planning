# Meal Plan: Week of 2026-01-20

## Sunday Prep (2-hour session)

- [ ] Chop Veggies: Zucchini (diced), Broccoli (florets), Asparagus (trim), Bell Peppers (strips).
- [ ] Prepare Meatballs: Mix ground turkey, breadcrumbs, and egg; form into balls.
- [ ] Prepare Mousse: Blend mango and coconut cream; refrigerate.
- [ ] Bake Oat Rusks: Mix oat flour, banana, and coconut oil; bake until firm.

## Weekly Highlights

- **Snack 1:** Pears with Almond Butter
- **Snack 2:** Hummus with Bell Pepper strips
- **Snack 3:** Apple slices with Peanut Butter
- **Dessert:** Mango Coconut Mousse with Oat Rusks
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 cup Frozen mango chunks (thawed)
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Full-fat coconut milk (canned)
    - [ ] 1 cup Rolled oats (for rusks)
    - [ ] 1 Ripe banana (for rusks)
    - [ ] 2 tbsp Coconut oil (for rusks)
  - **Preparation:**
        1. **Mousse:** Blend mango chunks and coconut milk until smooth and airy. Refrigerate for 2+ hours.
        2. **Oat Rusks:** Pulse 1 cup oats in a blender to make flour. Mix with 1 mashed banana and 2 tbsp melted coconut oil.
        3. Shape into finger-sized sticks and bake at 350°F for 15-20 mins until golden and firm.
        4. Serve mousse chilled with rusks for dipping.

## Tuesday

- **Dinner:** Turkey Meatballs with Whole Wheat Pasta and Zucchini
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1/2 lb Ground turkey
    - [ ] 1/4 cup Whole wheat breadcrumbs
    - [ ] 1 Egg
    - [ ] 1 can (14.5 oz) No-salt-added diced tomatoes
    - [ ] 2 tbsp Tomato paste (no salt added)
    - [ ] 1/2 tsp Dried oregano
    - [ ] 1/4 tsp Garlic powder
    - [ ] 4 oz Whole wheat pasta
    - [ ] 2 Zucchinis (diced)
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Mix turkey, breadcrumbs, and egg. Form small meatballs.
        2. Sauté meatballs in a pan until browned and cooked through (10-12 mins).
        3. Add diced zucchini to the same pan and sauté with the meatballs for 3-4 mins until tender.
        4. Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and garlic powder.
        5. Simmer for 10 mins to thicken the sauce and let flavors meld.
        6. Boil pasta according to package instructions. Drain and toss with meatballs, zucchini, and sauce.

## Wednesday

- **Breakfast:** Banana Peanut Butter Oatmeal
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Rolled oats
    - [ ] 1 cup Water
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Peanut butter
    - [ ] 1/2 Banana, sliced
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Cook oats in water (5-7 mins).
        2. Stir in peanut butter and top with banana.
- **Lunch:** (Leftovers from Tuesday Dinner)
- **Dinner:** High-Speed Shrimp Tacos
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1/2 lb Shrimp (peeled and deveined)
    - [ ] 4 Whole wheat tortillas
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Shredded cabbage
    - [ ] 1/4 cup Shredded cheese (low sodium)
    - [ ] 1/2 tsp Cumin
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Sauté shrimp with cumin in a pan for 3-4 mins until pink.
        2. Warm tortillas in a separate pan.
        3. Assemble tacos with shrimp, cabbage, and cheese.
  - _Note:_ This meal takes less than 15 minutes.

## Thursday

- **Breakfast:** Almond Butter Toast with Greek Yogurt
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 slice Whole wheat bread
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Almond butter
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Plain Greek yogurt
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Toast bread and spread with almond butter.
        2. Serve yogurt on the side.
- **Lunch:** (Leftovers from Wednesday Dinner)
- **Dinner:** Lemon Garlic Chicken with Asparagus and Cherry Tomatoes
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 lb Chicken breast, sliced into strips
    - [ ] 1 bunch Asparagus, trimmed
    - [ ] 1 cup Cherry tomatoes
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Olive oil
    - [ ] 1/2 Lemon, juiced
    - [ ] 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Sauté chicken strips in olive oil over medium-high heat until browned and cooked through (8-10 mins).
        2. Remove chicken and set aside.
        3. Add asparagus and cherry tomatoes to the pan. Sauté for 5-7 mins until asparagus is tender-crisp and tomatoes start to blister.
        4. Return chicken to the pan, add lemon juice and garlic powder, and toss to combine for 1-2 mins.

## Friday

- **Breakfast:** Scrambled Eggs with Spinach
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 2 Eggs
    - [ ] Handful of fresh spinach
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Sauté spinach in a greased pan until wilted.
        2. Add whisked eggs and scramble until firm.
- **Lunch:** (Leftovers from Thursday Dinner)
- **Dinner:** (Eat Out)

## Saturday

- **Breakfast:** Banana Pancakes
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 Ripe banana
    - [ ] 1 Egg
    - [ ] 2 tbsp Whole wheat flour
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Mash banana; whisk in egg and flour.
        2. Cook small pancakes on a greased pan (2 mins per side).
- **Lunch:** (Eat Out)
- **Dinner:** Beef and Broccoli Stir-fry with Edamame
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1/2 lb Beef strips (sirloin or flank)
    - [ ] 1/2 lb Shrimp
    - [ ] 2 cups Broccoli florets
    - [ ] 1/2 cup Shelled edamame
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Coconut aminos
    - [ ] 1 tsp Sesame oil
    - [ ] 1 tsp Sesame seeds
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Sauté beef and shrimp in sesame oil until browned.
        2. Add broccoli and edamame; sauté for 5-7 mins with a splash of water.
        3. Stir in coconut aminos and top with sesame seeds.

## Sunday

- **Breakfast:** Greek Yogurt with Fresh Berries
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 cup Plain Greek yogurt
    - [ ] 1/4 cup Mixed berries
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Mix berries into yogurt. Mash berries for baby.
- **Lunch:** (Leftovers from Saturday Dinner)
- **Dinner:** Roast Pork Tenderloin with Sweet Potatoes
  - **Ingredients:**
    - [ ] 1 lb Pork tenderloin
    - [ ] 2 Sweet potatoes, cubed
    - [ ] 1 tbsp Olive oil
    - [ ] 1/2 tsp Dried thyme
  - **Preparation:**
        1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
        2. Toss pork and sweet potatoes with oil and thyme.
        3. Roast for 20-25 mins until pork reaches 145°F.
