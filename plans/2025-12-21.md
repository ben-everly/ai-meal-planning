## Weekly Dessert

- **Dessert:** Mixed Berry Compote with Greek Yogurt
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 2 cups mixed frozen berries
        - 1 tbsp lemon juice
        - 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
        - 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (for adults only)
    - **Preparation:**
        1. In a small saucepan, simmer berries and lemon juice over medium heat for 10-15 minutes until berries break down and sauce thickens.
        2. Let cool.
        3. Store in the fridge. Serve over Greek yogurt, adding sweetener for adults.
           _Note: Serve baby plain yogurt with a small amount of the unsweetened berry compote._

## Sunday (Dec 21)

- **Dinner:** Roast Chicken with Sweet Potatoes and Broccoli
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 1 whole chicken (approx 3-4 lbs)
        - 2 large sweet potatoes, cubed
        - 2 heads of broccoli, cut into florets
        - 3 tbsp olive oil
        - 1 tsp dried thyme
        - 1 tsp dried rosemary
        - 1/2 tsp black pepper
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
        2. Place chicken in roasting pan. Arrange sweet potatoes around it.
        3. Drizzle with oil and sprinkle with herbs and pepper.
        4. Roast for 30 minutes.
        5. Add broccoli, roast for another 15-20 minutes until chicken is 165°F.
    - _Note:_ Save leftovers for Monday and Tuesday lunch.

## Monday (Dec 22)

- **Breakfast:** Peanut Butter and Banana Oatmeal (Peanut, Dairy)
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 1/2 cup rolled oats
        - 1 cup milk of choice (or water)
        - 1 tbsp natural peanut butter
        - 1/2 banana, sliced or mashed
        - Dash of cinnamon
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Combine oats and milk in a pot.
        2. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until soft.
        3. Stir in peanut butter and cinnamon.
        4. Top with banana.
- **Lunch:** (Leftovers from Sunday Dinner)
- **Dinner:** Salmon with Quinoa and Peas (Fish)
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 2 salmon fillets
        - 1 cup quinoa
        - 1 cup frozen peas
        - 1 tbsp olive oil
        - Lemon wedges
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Cook quinoa in 2 cups water for 15 mins. Add peas in last 5 mins.
        2. Sear salmon in oil for 3-4 minutes per side until cooked through.
- **Sides:** Steamed Broccoli

## Friday (Dec 26)

- **Breakfast:** Apple Cinnamon Porridge
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 1/2 cup Oats
        - 1/2 Apple, grated
        - 1 cup water or milk
        - Dash of cinnamon
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
        2. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until soft.
- **Lunch:** Leftover Turkey Sandwiches
    - (Repeat from Tuesday Dinner)
- **Dinner:** Beef & Veggie Stir-fry (Soy)
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 1/2 lb Ground beef or thinly sliced steak
        - Mixed bell peppers and snap peas (pre-chopped Sunday)
        - 1 tbsp Coconut aminos (low sodium alternative to soy sauce)
        - 1 tsp Ginger, minced
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Brown beef in a large pan.
        2. Add veggies and sauté for 5 minutes.
        3. Stir in ginger and coconut aminos.
        4. Serve over remaining quinoa or rice.

## Saturday (Dec 27)

- **Breakfast:** Banana Pancakes (Egg, Wheat)
    - **Ingredients:**
        - 1 ripe banana
        - 1 egg
        - 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Mash banana, mix with egg and flour.
        2. Cook small spoonfuls on a greased pan for 2 mins per side.
- **Lunch:** (Eat Out)
- **Dinner:** Pasta with Marinara and White Beans (Wheat, Soy)
    - **Ingredients:**
        - Whole wheat pasta
        - Low-sodium marinara sauce
        - 1/2 cup White beans (cannellini), rinsed
    - **Preparation:**
        1. Boil pasta according to package.
        2. Warm sauce and beans in a separate pan.
        3. Mix and serve.
